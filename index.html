The Cross-Collegiate Minecraft League is an ever-growing network of university and college Minecraft Clubs that hosts North America-wide Minecraft events! In February 2024, we partnered with MrTechnodad to host Spud Showdown, a charity event that raised over $2,600 USD / $3,500 CAD for the One of Us Global Foundation!
